Training Phase

Before you begin moderating other participants, you'll complete a short training phase to familiarize yourself with the dot-counting task.

Training Overview:
  • You'll complete {{ Constants.training_rounds }} practice rounds
  • In each round, you'll see dots for {{ Constants.dots_display_seconds }} seconds
  • Your task is to determine which side has more dots
  • You need to answer correctly at least {{ Constants.min_training_correct }} times to proceed
Remember:
  • The dots are divided by a diagonal line
  • Count quickly but carefully
  • You'll get feedback after each training round
